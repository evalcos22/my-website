HEALING SUNCHOKES



Sunchokes are the source for loads of positive health effects. Sunchokes are high in inulin which suppors digestive health and weight management. They also help you control your blood sugar which helps manage your diabetes or insulin sensitivity. Sunchokes are super rich in minerals like iron and magnesium. Sunchokes are also super low in calories. The best health effect it has though is supporting heart health by maintaining good cholesterol and blood pressure. It is one of the healthiest vegetables that you could eat and can be a part of a good diet.
